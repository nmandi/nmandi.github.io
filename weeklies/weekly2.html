Rand believed that all people should be thinking independently and living selfishly in a way, in order to be rational. It lead to a lot of innovation influenced entrepreneurs and creators in Silicon Valley quite a bit, giving birth to Californian ideology. Californian ideology was the idea that all people could be Ayn Rand heroes bc of the order created by computers and machines. It allowed for connections and networks between human beings which created a baseline structure. However, if all people lived as Ayn Rand desired, they could live independently while still having connection and order within society. The idea was demonstrated as potentially possible by the red/green collective Pong game by Loren and Rachel Carpenter.
